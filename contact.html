Frequently Asked Questions


We provide services including: intimate in-home wine tastings, public wine events, and WINEtending services. Our only product is the WINE box.

We are not a wine brand, nor do we sell individual bottles of wine due to PA licensing laws.

However, please explore our partners page to see some brands we feature. You can purchase directly from them, and certain events we hold will have discount codes applicable to our partners.

We exclusively feature black-owned wine brands in all our products and services, celebrating diversity in the world of wine. We also focus on introducing Pittsburgh locals to wines that are not sold in Pittsburgh.

WINEtending is our personalized wine service where we bring the tasting experience to your doorstep. You can customize your selection and enjoy a curated wine experience at your event.
